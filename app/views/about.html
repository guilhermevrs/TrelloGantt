Trello GANTT is an initiative created by WING - Taking Your Further Projects.

The TG (as we called Trello GANTT) was created to supply a very specific need for managing projects at WING. But as the tool was quite functional, its creators Guilherme Schwade and Rodrigo Donini decided to publish it to other people and companies would benefit from this awseome way to view lists and tasks of Trello.

Trello GANTT is a open source project, and if you would like to contribute it, fork in Github
